About Boredless Games

You know those moments when you are with people who want to play a game, and you have certain items to use (or not), but you have no clue what to play? That's where Boredless Games comes in. It is a database for all of those games that doesn't require anything specialized. No boards required. Only, say, a deck of cards, some dice, a pen and paper, or perhaps nothing at all.

From solitaire card games to competitive group games, from hilarious drinking games to parlour games to pass the time, Boredless Games will help you figure out what to play. Browse the full gallery of games or use our advanced search feature to narrow it down to your best matches.

Don't see a game that should be included? Please fill out the suggest a game form and let us know about it!

About The Creators

Boredless Games was created by the web design duo of Erin Ternes (designer) and John Michael Ternes (developer).

Aside from being partners on this project, they are also siblings (the oldest and youngest of five) so they have many years of working on all sorts of projects — and playing countless games — together.
